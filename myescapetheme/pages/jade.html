Jade X Fleishhacker

Jade was born in Guangdong Province, China with the given name of Zhu Xinhui. At five she was adopted by an American family, attaining her U.S. citizenship, and then moved to Amsterdam. A couple of years later, she moved to New Jersey. Upon going to seventh grade, she and her family moved to California. She now lives and goes to school in San Francisco. She is majoring in Web Design & New Media for her BFA.
