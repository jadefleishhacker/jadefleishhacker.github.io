CLASSES

Tiger Class

(4-6 yrs old)

Tuesday - Thursday

3:30 - 4:15

Tiger class will learn control of their body, respect for themselves, their fellow classmates, and instructors. They will begin with basic falls, kicks, stretches, and techniques/forms.

Kids Class

(4-6 yrs old)

Monday - Friday

4:20 - 5:20

Here we will continue will learn control of their body, respect and discipline. They will continue building their knowledge in kicks as well as falls in the best way to not injure themselves.

Teenager Class

(4-6 yrs old)

Monday - Friday

5:30 - 6:30

Building on top, the teens will be focusing on stamina and strength activites as well as movement, stances, evading and ground positions. They are now highly reccommended to take part in the friday class to practice sparring.

Adult Class

(4-6 yrs old)

Monday - Friday

7:30 - 8:30

Here we will continue to train both the body and mind. We will increase pace in our lessons and going more in depth in techniques, forms, etc.

Saturday Open Class

(7 and up)

Saturday

10:00 - 11:00

This class will study in controlling the body, movement and stepping. We will go through more advance sets of kicking and combinations.
