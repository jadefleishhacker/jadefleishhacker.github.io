COVID - 19 PLANS

Hi everyone,

As we are going through these challenging times with so much uncertainty, things are happening that are not in our control. One thing we cannot forget is who we are… We are the Yang's Martial Arts Academy! We are a community! A community supports one another and works through tough times together! To this point, it is important that we keep some sense of normalcy. It is also very important that we teach by example how we want our children to face difficult circumstances in their future. We at Yang's Martial Arts are adapting and adjusting to our circumstances to ensure we are here to serve you our Yang's Martial Arts families.

We are teaching online classes! During this first week, I have been working out the technical issues with our online classes. We will be adjusting our class schedule to accommodate our students’ normal routine as much as possible.
