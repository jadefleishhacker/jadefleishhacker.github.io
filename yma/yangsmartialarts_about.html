ABOUT US

HISTORY

Kung Jung Mu Sul is Korean for Traditional Royal Court Martial Arts. A lethal system of self-defense whose origins can be traced back to the three original martial arts systems of Korea. Kung Jung Mu Sul - Royal Court Martial Arts. Bulkyo Mu Sul - Buddhist Martial Arts. Sado Mu Sul - Tribal Martial Arts. Kung Jung Mu Sul is a comprehensive martial arts system dealing with both single and multiple attackers utilizing a vast array of kicking, in-close striking, joint lock manipulation, grappling and weapon techniques from both the standing and ground positions.

MEET THE GRAND MASTER

Grand Master Soon Tae Yang

9th degree Kung Jung Mu Sul Grandmaster Soon Tae Yang President and founder of the International Kung Jung Mu Sul Federation. 9th Degree Black Belt in Tae Kwon Do, representing the Korean Tae Kwon Do Association. Master Yang has devoted his life to becoming an expert in all aspects of martial arts and is especially renown for his mastery of weapons, intricate kicking, joint manipulation techniques and ki development. Master Yang has been featured in Karate Illustrated, Taekwondo, and Karate/Kungfu magazines and has been featured on many different television programs. Touring the United States, Central America, Asia and Europe, he is renown throughout the world for his dynamic demonstrations and seminars.
