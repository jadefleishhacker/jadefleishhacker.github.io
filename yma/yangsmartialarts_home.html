YANG'S MARTIAL ARTS

KUNG JUNG MU SUL

Traditional Korean Royal Court Martial Arts

JOIN NOW

A practitioner of Kung Jung Mu Sul will be exposed to fighting and defending oneself against both single and multiple attackers from kicking to in-close striking, grabbing and grappling defense both from standing up and on-the-ground positions. Kung Jung Mu Sul practitioners also receive exposure and training in a variety of weapons. (sword, staff, rope, knife, walking cane, fan, short sticks, short swords, ax, and nunchucks)
