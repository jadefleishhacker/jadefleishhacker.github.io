Team

HEAVEN ZHANG

Founder & CEO

Heaven was born in China and then moved to Australia. When she lived in China, it was tough for her to gain experience as a Photographer's assistant due to gender inequality. However when she moved to Australia she was able to take classes and develop her own style and have control over her own studio. She then moved to California to study at the Academy of Arts University in San Francisco. Here she continued her studies for another 4 years. She before graduation she started the heavenzjpg photography company.

JADE FLEISHHACKER

Photographer's Assistant

Jade was also born in China but then moved to the Netherlands when she was young. After moving to the East coast of the U.S., she became interested in the art of photography. However, it was until she moved to California that she was able to study photography. She studied in high school for a couple of years and then studied in the Academy of Arts University under the Photography major. There she met Heaven and joined her in 2018.
